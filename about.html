About Domovoi

Domovoi is a project by Eric Turner, who needed a better way to run his madhouse of automation than a slew of hastily assembled applications and sloppy interfaces. Having played "Quest for Glory: Shadows of Darkness" more than any adolescent permissably should, the idea of a Domovoi, a slavic house spirit who protects the home, seemed like a great anology for his mad software schemes.
