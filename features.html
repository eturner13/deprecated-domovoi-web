Powerful Management

A room with a view...

Sure, there's a lot that Domovoi can control, and too many controls can easily get confusing. But what if there was a simple way to manage that power in a way that makes perfect sense? Introducing Domovoi's Room View, a sleek interface that organizes your home the way you do: by the room. Each room is individually represented with controls specfic to the devices relevant to the area. No need to dig through menus to make simple or complex tweaks, and multiple rooms can be controlled in paralel.

Scheduling and Events

A time for change...

There's not much that can be more gratifying than when things just work, and work without having to be constantly managed. Domovoi's scheduling feature is a painless way to organize the small but important details of managing a home. Set schemes for various times of the day, such as less lighting during daylight hours, or mood lighting for when the hour strikes late. Set cameras to record or sense motion only during selected hours. Use schemes to set the perfect tone for the spontaneous event, or as a response to a triggered event. Walking to the kitchen for a late night snack? Let Domovoi light your path as you travel through the home, and stop becoming the thing that goes bump in the night.

Lights

Setting the scene...

Countless studies have shown that as a species, we're very affected by the light environment (or light pollution in some cases) that we surround ourselves with. At night, intense light can produce unwanted sleep issues. Productivity can be dampened with harsh flourescent lighting. Waking up to a circadian rhythm of soft blue light gradually changing to sunlight can help make mornings paced, graceful and, for some, tolerable. Of course, sometimes we just want to dance to disco light show. Or a comforting, toe-stub preventing night-light. Domovoi makes full use of the latest in network connected LED lights, changing brightness, colors and color temperature with ease, and can even work with LED strip lighting for the ultimate dazzling light show.

Cameras

A watchful eye...

Keeping watch over your home is a job Domovoi takes seriously, and it has the tools to get the job done. Capable of recording constantly or only when motion is detected, Domovoi lets you choose the options that work for your lifestyle needs and storage capacities. Get alerts when Domovoi detects something, or have it respond with lighting, audio or other connected devices.
