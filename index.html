Domovoi is everywhere

Domovoi is built to be used across a variety of devices

Built from the ground up for the mobile web, Domovoi makes any almost any device with a web browser into a home control interface. Domovoi lets you watch your home's IP cameras, adjust wifi lighting and much more from the convenience of your phone, tablet, laptop, or a wide selection of devices with browser capabilities.

Domovoi is powerful

Let Domovoi take control over your home's schedule

Domovoi can save you time and money by scheduling your home's functionality. With intuitive scheduling features, Domovoi takes control of when lights are on or off, cameras are watching for movement, networked appliances are operating and more. Spend less time making sure the lights are turned off at night, pay less at the meter when nobody's home, and never have to think twice about it.

Or create events for Domovoi to watch for. Have Domovoi brighten the lights when someone rings the doorbell, or perform any number of responses that Domovoi understands. Having an impromptu party? Setup a scene so that Domovoi takes care of cranking the lights and disco music so that you can spend more time getting turnt up.
