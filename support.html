F.A.Q. - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Domovoi?

Domovoi is a web-based application that makes home automation easy, controlling lights, cameras, media sources and external networked devices and appliances.

What is *A* Domovoi?

In slavic cultures, a Domovoi is a friendly house spirit that watched over the family home. Read about them here!

What do I need to use Domovoi?

You'll need compatible devices (a list is available on the hardware page), starting with at least one networked camera.

What does a subscription provide?

A Domovoi account can control a single property locally (no internet access), but a monthly subscription gives remote access (via the internet), gives other Domovoi users selective control as a guest at your discretion, and allows control over an unlimited number of properties.
